IDEATHON ? ROUND 1 PROBLEM STATEMENT

Design a technology-enabled solution that addresses a meaningful real-world problem and can create measurable, scalable impact.

Your submission should clearly explain:
1. The problem and who experiences it.
2. Your proposed solution and its key differentiator.
3. The technology or AI approach you would use.
4. The expected impact and a realistic path to scale.
5. The most important features in a first working prototype.

Round 1 deliverables:
- Completed online ideation form
- Pitch deck (PDF or PPTX)
- Optional architecture diagram, demo link, repository, or supporting document

Evaluation focus:
- Problem clarity
- Originality and innovation
- Feasibility
- Responsible use of technology
- Potential impact and scalability
